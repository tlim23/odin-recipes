Hot Dog Recipe

A quick and easy hot dog recipe!

This hot dog recipe is quick and easy!

Ingredients

  • 8 hot dogs, chopped
  • ⅔ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 3 tablespoons pickle relish
  • 3 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons prepared mustard
  • 3 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 8 hot dog buns
